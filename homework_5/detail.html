Product Details


This cat collar is literally the best invention in the ENTIRE WORLD. We here at US Cat Supplies love cats so much that we followed our own around for 12 weeks with our hands as collars to learn where cats put the most tension on a harness so that we could design something great. Also, the GPS is great for tracking when your teenage kitty is sneaking out to go steal treats from the neighbors.


Our collar fits cats from the size of your palm all the way to those big chonky bois that can`t stop eating treats. You can find our size chart on our website or just guess like most people (medium is a safe bet!).


No cats were harmed in the testing of our products but we cannot say the same for our employees.
